Course Curriculum

This curriculum provides a comprehensive introduction to digital design, focusing on foundational skills in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere, and Animate. Students will develop their creativity and technical expertise through hands-on projects that explore design principles, digital tools, and creative problem-solving. By the end of the course, students will demonstrate proficiency in creating visually engaging and professional-level designs, animations, and multimedia projects.

Lesson Plan

The Menu Design lesson introduces students to Adobe Photoshop, emphasizing essential tools and techniques such as layers, typography, alignment, and visual hierarchy. Through this project, students learn how to create a professional and visually appealing menu design while applying design principles like balance, contrast, and unity. By completing this project, students will demonstrate their ability to combine technical skills with creative thinking to produce a polished and functional design.

Student Resources

This project includes comprehensive student resources such as step-by-step Instructions and additional resources like Design Tips and a Help Guide. In class instruction, I also show several previous student examples and professional examples for the students to visually understand the project. These resources are designed to support diverse learning styles and help students build confidence in their design skills while working through the menu design project.
